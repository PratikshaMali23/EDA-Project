# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis

##📌 Project Overview:

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by businesses, as losing customers directly impacts revenue and growth. This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a customer churn dataset to identify the key factors influencing customer retention and churn.

The analysis includes data cleaning, visualization, feature exploration, and business insights using Python libraries. Various charts and graphs are used to uncover hidden patterns and trends that help understand customer behavior.

##🎯 Objectives:

Understand customer demographics and behavior.

Identify the major reasons behind customer churn.

Analyze relationships between different customer attributes.

Discover patterns using visualizations.

Generate actionable business insights for improving customer retention.

##🔍 Key Insights:

Customers with month-to-month contracts are more likely to churn.

Customers with higher monthly charges tend to have a higher churn rate.

Long-tenure customers are generally more loyal.

Electronic check payment users show relatively higher churn.

Contract type significantly impacts customer retention.

Customers with additional services are less likely to churn.

Internet service type also influences churn behavior.

##💼 Business Recommendations:

Encourage customers to switch from month-to-month contracts to long-term contracts.

Improve customer engagement during the early months of subscription.

Provide personalized offers to high-risk customers.

Enhance customer support services.

Monitor customers with high monthly charges and offer loyalty benefits.
